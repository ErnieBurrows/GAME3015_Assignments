\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Simple first-\/person camera class for 3D scene navigation. 

Maintains camera coordinate system and viewing frustum to generate view/projection matrices. Provides movement controls (strafe/walk/pedestal) and rotation controls (pitch/yaw/roll).
